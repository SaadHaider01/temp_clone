Stainless Steel Equipment Manufacturers

Unitech are a leading UK manufacturer of high quality stainless steel and aluminium equipment. We pride ourselves on our outstanding level of quality, regularly supplying products to a wide range of market sectors including the food, beverage, pharmaceutical and other hygiene conscious industries. As the UAE subsidiary, Unitech Stailness Steel Specialist Engineering L.L.C carries forward this legacy, delivering the same standards of excellence and specialised engineering solutions across the United Arab Emirates and the wider GCC region.
