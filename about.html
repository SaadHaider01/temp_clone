Unitech Engineering is a leading supplier of high-quality stainless steel and aluminium equipment to the food, beverage, pharmaceutical and other hygiene conscious industries.

UNITECH STAINLESS STEEL SPECIALIST ENGINEERING L.L.C is a key subsidiary of the Unitech Group, operating in the United Arab Emirates and building upon the strong foundation established by Unitech Engineering Ltd, the group’s largest business formed in 1991. Leveraging the group’s extensive expertise and heritage, we deliver high-quality stainless steel and aluminium engineering solutions tailored to the needs of the regional market. From our base in the UAE, we provide full turnkey projects, supported by advanced manufacturing capabilities and a commitment to excellence, serving clients across diverse sectors with the same standards that define the Unitech name worldwide.
